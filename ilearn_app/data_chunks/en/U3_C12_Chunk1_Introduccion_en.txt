- LO 2: Apply the order of operations in calculations that combine addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division, including the use of parentheses.

Overview:
This chapter develops the ability to solve mathematical expressions with more than one operation, respecting the order of operations: first parentheses, then multiplication/division, and finally addition/subtraction.
