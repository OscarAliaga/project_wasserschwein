Explanation:
Translation is a movement of a figure without rotating or deforming it. It moves a distance in a straight line in one direction.

Example:
Moving a triangle 3 squares to the right and 2 down on a grid plane.

Activity:
a) Draw a figure on grid paper
b) Perform a translation of 2 units to the left and 3 up
c) Describe the movements using coordinates (x, y)
