Title: Problems with length units

Description: Solve the following problems applying what you have learned about length measurements.

1. Sofia ran 2.3 km in the morning and 1.7 km in the afternoon. How many meters did she run in total during the day?
2. A ribbon measures 350 centimeters. If it is cut into pieces of 70 centimeters each, how many equal pieces can be made?
3. Juan needs to join two boards. One measures 125 cm and the other 1.45 m. What is the total length of the joined board, in meters?

Analysis question:
When is it convenient to use meters instead of centimeters or millimeters? Explain in your own words.

Tip: Before solving, convert all lengths to the same unit to make it easier to operate.
