- LO 5: Understand decimal numbers up to thousandths

Overview:
In this chapter, students delve into the meaning of decimal numbers, their relationship with fractions, reading, writing, and decomposition. Addition and subtraction of decimals are also practiced, emphasizing the alignment of the decimal point. Activities include representation on the number line and the use of real contexts such as measurements or money.
