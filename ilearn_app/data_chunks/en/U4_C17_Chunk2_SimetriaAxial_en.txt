Explanation:
A figure is symmetrical if it can be divided into two equal parts that are mirror images of each other with respect to an axis (line of symmetry).

Examples:
- A butterfly
- Logos of some brands
- Letters like A, M, T

Activity:
a) Draw figures with horizontal and vertical symmetry
b) Mark the axis of symmetry in each figure
c) What figures from everyday surroundings are symmetrical?
