Explanation:
- A proper fraction has the numerator smaller than the denominator. Example: 2/5
- An improper fraction has the numerator greater than or equal to the denominator. Example: 7/4

Improper fractions can also be expressed as mixed numbers.

Example:
7/4 = 1 3/4

Activity:
a) Draw a figure and represent 3/4  
b) Represent 5/3 as an improper fraction and as a mixed number  
c) Classify: 2/3, 6/6, 9/8, 4/2 as proper or improper fractions
