Situations:
1. One pencil measures 12.5 cm. Another measures 7.8 cm. How long are they together?
2. A bottle contains 1.25 L. If 0.4 L are consumed, how much is left?
3. A student read 0.75 pages on Monday and 1.2 on Tuesday. How many pages did they read in total?

Activity:
Solve the problems, aligning the decimals correctly. Then represent the result on a number line when possible.

Reflection:
Why is it important to know how to add and subtract decimals in everyday contexts such as money or measurements?
