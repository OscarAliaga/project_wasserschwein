Explanation:
- An impossible event cannot occur (probability = 0)
- A possible event can occur, although not with certainty
- A certain event always occurs (probability = 1)

Examples:
- Rolling a number greater than 6 on a die: impossible
- Drawing a red shape from a bag with various colored shapes: possible
- The sun will rise tomorrow: certain
