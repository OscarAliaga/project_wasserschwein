Explanation:
Two lines are parallel if they maintain the same distance between them and never intersect, even if extended.

Examples:
- Opposite sides of a rectangle
- Train tracks

Activity:
a) Draw a rectangle and mark the parallel sides
b) Observe your classroom and look for parallel structures (e.g. edges of the blackboard)
c) Use a ruler to verify if two lines are parallel
