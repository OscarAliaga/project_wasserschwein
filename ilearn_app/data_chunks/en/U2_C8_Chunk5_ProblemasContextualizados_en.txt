Situations:
1. A recipe requires 3/4 cup of sugar. If you only have a 1/4 cup measure, how many should you use?
2. I read 5/8 of the book this week and 1/4 last week. Did I read more this week?
3. There is 2/5 of a pizza left. If I share it between 2 people, how much does each person get?

Activity:
a) Graphically represent the data from the problems
b) Justify the answers with addition or subtraction of fractions
c) Comment on why it is useful to understand fractions in cooking or reading
