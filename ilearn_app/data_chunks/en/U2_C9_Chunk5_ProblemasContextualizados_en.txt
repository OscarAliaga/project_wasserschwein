Scenarios:
1. A bar graph shows the number of bicycles sold per month. In which month were the most bicycles sold? What was the difference with the following month?
2. A table shows weekly school attendance. In which week was there the highest attendance?
3. A line graph shows the temperature in July. What trend do you observe?

Activity:
a) Create a graph based on a survey you conduct (favorite food, sport, etc.)
b) Exchange your graph with a classmate and interpret theirs
c) Write 2 conclusions you can draw from the observed data
