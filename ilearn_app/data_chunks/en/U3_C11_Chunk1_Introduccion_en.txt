- LO 17: Identify and describe events as certain, possible, or impossible in simple random experiments

Overview:
This chapter introduces the notion of probability as a tool to describe how likely an event is to occur. Games, roulette, dice, and other everyday situations are used to analyze and classify events as certain, possible, or impossible. Students are encouraged to argue based on their observations and predictions.
