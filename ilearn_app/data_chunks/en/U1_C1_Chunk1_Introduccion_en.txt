Chapter Overview:
In this chapter, students learn to represent, compose, and decompose natural numbers up to 9 digits (999,999,999). The positional value of the digits is worked on, and the comparison and ordering of numbers are practiced. It is contextualized with everyday situations, such as country populations, distances, or industrial production.
