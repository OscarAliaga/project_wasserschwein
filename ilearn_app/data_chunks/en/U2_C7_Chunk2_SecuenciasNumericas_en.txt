Explanation:
A numerical pattern is a sequence of numbers organized according to a rule. It can be:
- Increasing: 2, 4, 6, 8... (adding 2)
- Decreasing: 100, 90, 80... (subtracting 10)

Example:
Sequence: 5, 10, 15, 20  
Rule: add 5
