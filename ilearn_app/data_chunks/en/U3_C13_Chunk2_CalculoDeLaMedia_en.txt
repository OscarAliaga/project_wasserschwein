Explanation:
The mean is calculated by adding all the values and dividing by the number of elements.

Formula:
Mean = (sum of the data) / (number of data points)

Example:
Grades: 5, 6, 4  
Mean = (5 + 6 + 4) / 3 = 15 / 3 = 5
