Title: Converting length units

Description: Observe the following lengths and convert them to the indicated unit.

1. 3 meters = ________ centimeters
2. 1.5 kilometers = ________ meters
3. 200 millimeters = ________ centimeters
4. 4.25 meters = ________ millimeters

Analysis question:
What pattern do you observe between the units you are converting? What do you need to do to go from a larger unit to a smaller one?

Student instructions:
- Use the unit conversion table.
- Remember that:
  - 1 meter = 100 centimeters
  - 1 kilometer = 1,000 meters
  - 1 centimeter = 10 millimeters
