Explanation:
Triangles are classified according to the length of their sides:
- Equilateral: three equal sides
- Isosceles: two equal sides
- Scalene: all sides different

Activity:
a) Draw one triangle of each type
b) Measure the sides with a ruler and confirm the classification
c) Observe structures in your environment that appear to have triangles and classify them
